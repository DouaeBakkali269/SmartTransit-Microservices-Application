SERVICE: Ticket Service
DESCRIPTION: Manages bookings, ticket generation, QR codes, and validation.

OWNED DATA (SCHEMAS):
- Bookings
- Tickets
- TicketValidations
- ExchangeHistory

API ENDPOINTS:
POST /api/v1/bookings
    - Creates a "Pending" booking.
GET /api/v1/bookings/{bookingId}
GET /api/v1/bookings/{bookingId}/validate
POST /api/v1/bookings/calculate-price
    - Logic to sum ticket prices + apply discounts.

GET /api/v1/tickets
    - User's tickets.
GET /api/v1/tickets/{ticketId}
POST /api/v1/tickets/{ticketId}/exchange
POST /api/v1/tickets/{ticketId}/exchange-eligibility
DELETE /api/v1/tickets/{ticketId}
    - Cancel/Refund logic.
GET /api/v1/tickets/{ticketId}/qr-code
GET /api/v1/tickets/{ticketId}/download
POST /api/v1/tickets/validate
    - Used by scanning devices/Driver app.
POST /api/v1/tickets/generate
    - Finalize ticket after payment.

GET /api/v1/admin/dashboard
    - Aggregates Revenue data and Ticket sales for dashboard.
GET /api/v1/admin/dashboard/realtime
    - Recent bookings.

KAFKA EVENTS:
- Consumes: `payment.success` (Triggers ticket generation).
- Produces: `booking.created`, `ticket.issued`, `ticket.cancelled`, `ticket.validated`.
